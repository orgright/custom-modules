Committees

The orgRight system provides that opportunity to define any committees and sub-committees that have been established by your organisation. A committee may be known by another term such as "board" or "forum", each of which has a general purpose of essentially providing a multi-person unit to oversee, manage, or undertake activities. The orgRight system provides the capability to manage members serving on each committee, and which roles if any, an individual has. Committees may be linked to meetings as a record of their activities.
